Sales Executive

Incentive-Based Role

Job Description

MHB Tech Solutions is looking for a motivated and result-driven Sales Executive to help expand our client base and promote our software and IT services.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify potential clients and generate leads
  • Pitch company services and software solutions
  • Understand client requirements and propose suitable solutions
  • Close deals and maintain client relationships
  • Coordinate with technical team when required

Eligibility & Skills

  • Good communication and negotiation skills
  • Basic understanding of software and IT services
  • Self-motivated and target-oriented mindset
  • Freshers and experienced candidates both can apply

Compensation (Important)

This is a 100% incentive-based role. You will earn commission on every successfully closed deal.

  • No fixed salary
  • Attractive incentives per client acquisition
  • Unlimited earning potential based on performance

Job Details

  • Job Type: Sales / Business Development
  • Location: Aurangabad / Remote
  • Experience: 0–2 Years
  • Joining: Immediate

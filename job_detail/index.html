Sales Executive

Incentive-Based Role | Aurangabad & Remote

Job Description & Opportunities

MHB Tech Solutions is looking for a motivated and result-driven Sales Executive to help expand our client base. This role is crucial for our growth as we promote high-end software and data analytics services to businesses across India.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify potential business clients through market research and lead generation tools.
  • Pitch comprehensive software solutions, including web development and data engineering services.
  • Conduct product demonstrations for "MY GYM Management" and "INVENTO Inventory" software.
  • Understand unique client bottlenecks and propose customized tech solutions.
  • Close high-value deals and maintain long-term professional relationships.

Eligibility & Skills

  • Excellent communication, persuasion, and negotiation skills.
  • Foundational understanding of modern software products and IT services.
  • Target-oriented mindset with the ability to work independently.
  • Open to both fresh graduates and experienced sales professionals.

Compensation Details

Please note that this is a 100% incentive-based role. Earnings are directly tied to your performance and successful client acquisitions.

  • High-commission structure per successfully closed deal.
  • No ceiling on your earning potential—the more you close, the more you earn.
  • Additional bonuses for reaching quarterly performance milestones.

General Information

  • Department: Business Development
  • Location: Hybrid (Aurangabad Office / Remote)
  • Work Schedule: Flexible hours based on client meetings.
  • Joining Date: Immediate openings available.

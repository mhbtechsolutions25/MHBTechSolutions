Invento

Smart Inventory & Billing Software for Growing Businesses

What is Invento?

Invento is a simple and reliable business management software designed for shops, stores, and small-to-medium enterprises. It helps business owners keep track of inventory, billing, customers, staff, and daily operations from one place.

Invento focuses on clarity, accuracy, and ease of use—allowing business owners to spend less time managing records and more time growing their business.

Key Features

  • Secure Login System – Role-based access to protect business data
  • Store Dashboard – Quick overview of sales, stock, and activity
  • Inventory Management – Track stock levels, products, and availability
  • Billing & Invoicing – Generate accurate bills quickly and efficiently
  • Staff Management – Manage staff access and activity
  • Customer Management – Store customer details and purchase history
  • Reports – Sales, stock, and business performance reports
  • Accounts Overview – Clear view of business financial records
